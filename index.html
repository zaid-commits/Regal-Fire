Frequently Asked Questions

How often should fire extinguishers be inspected?

Fire extinguishers should be inspected annually by a professional to ensure they are in proper working condition and have not been discharged or tampered with.

Do I need a fire alarm system for my business?

Most commercial buildings are required by law to have a fire alarm system installed. Our team can help you determine the specific requirements for your business and recommend the appropriate system.

What should I do in case of a fire emergency?

In the event of a fire emergency, remain calm, evacuate the premises immediately, and call emergency services. Do not attempt to fight the fire unless it is small and you have been properly trained.
