FAQs

How often should fire equipment be inspected?

Fire equipment should be inspected regularly, at least once a year, to ensure it is in proper working condition and compliant with safety regulations.

What types of fire alarms do you install?

We install a variety of fire alarms, including smoke detectors, heat detectors, and multi-sensor alarms to provide comprehensive fire detection coverage.
